Energy Monitoring

Our IoT-based energy monitoring offers you a comprehensive solution for real-time monitoring and analysis of your energy consumption. With state-of-the-art technology and intelligent sensors, you can identify savings potentials and optimize your energy efficiency.

  • Real-time data collection: Continuous monitoring of energy consumption and generation.
  • Data analysis: Identification of savings potentials and optimization opportunities.
  • User-friendly visualization: Intuitive dashboards for clear insights into your energy data.

Our solution is based on IoT technology, which enables seamless integration into existing systems. The collected data is transmitted in real time to our cloud-based platform, where it is analyzed and visualized.

With our energy monitoring you benefit from:

  • Cost savings:  Reduction of energy consumption through targeted optimization.
  • Transparency:  Clear insights into your energy consumption and generation.
  • Sustainability:  Contribution to the reduction of the CO₂ footprint through efficient energy use.

Rely on forward-looking technology to efficiently monitor and optimize your energy consumption. Contact us to learn more about our IoT solutions for energy monitoring.

Hydrometry

Our IoT-based hydrometry solution enables the precise collection and analysis of hydrological data such as water levels, flow rates, and precipitation. With state-of-the-art LoRaWAN technology and intelligent sensors, we offer you a reliable and efficient solution for monitoring water bodies and hydrological systems.

  • Real-time data collection: Continuous measurement of water levels, flow rates, and precipitation.
  • High-precision sensors: Use of highly accurate level probes and flow meters.
  • Energy efficiency: LoRaWAN devices with ultra-low power consumption for long-term deployments.

Our solution is based on LoRaWAN technology, which enables reliable and long-range data transmission even in remote areas. The collected data is transmitted in real time to our cloud-based platform, where it is analyzed and visualized.

With our hydrometry solution, you benefit from:

  • Improved data quality: Precise and reliable measurements for well-informed decisions.
  • Cost efficiency:  Low maintenance requirements thanks to robust and durable sensors.
  • Scalability:  Easy integration into existing infrastructures and expansion of the system.

Rely on future-oriented technology to efficiently collect and analyze your hydrological data. Contact us to learn more about our IoT solutions for hydrometry.

Flood Protection

Unser IoT-basierter Hochwasserschutz bietet Ihnen eine zuverlässige und effiziente Lösung zur Überwachung von Wasserständen und zur Früherkennung von Hochwassergefahren. Mit modernster LoRaWAN-Technologie und intelligenten Sensoren wie Pegelsonden schützen Sie Infrastruktur, Eigentum und Leben.

  • Echtzeit-Überwachung: Kontinuierliche Messung von Wasserständen durch präzise Pegelsonden.
  • Frühwarnsystem: Automatische Alarmierung bei kritischen Wasserständen.
  • Energieeffizienz: LoRaWAN-Geräte mit extrem niedrigem Energieverbrauch für langfristige Einsätze.

Unsere Lösung basiert auf der LoRaWAN-Technologie, die eine zuverlässige und weitreichende Datenübertragung auch in abgelegenen Gebieten ermöglicht. Die gesammelten Daten werden in Echtzeit an unsere cloudbasierte Plattform übertragen, wo sie analysiert und visualisiert werden.

Mit unserem Hochwasserschutz-System profitieren Sie von:

  • Minimierten Risiken:  Schnelle Reaktion auf steigende Wasserstände.
  • Kosteneffizienz:  Geringer Wartungsaufwand durch robuste und langlebige Sensoren.
  • Skalierbarkeit:  Einfache Integration in bestehende Infrastrukturen und Erweiterung des Systems.

Schützen Sie sich vor den verheerenden Auswirkungen von Hochwasser und setzen Sie auf eine zukunftsorientierte Technologie. Kontaktieren Sie uns, um mehr über unsere IoT-Lösungen für den Hochwasserschutz zu erfahren.

Gas Density Monitoring

Our IoT-based SF6 gas density monitoring provides a reliable solution for the continuous monitoring of sulfur hexafluoride (SF6) density in electrical systems. With state-of-the-art sensors and LoRaWAN technology, we ensure the safety and efficiency of your installations.

  • Real-time monitoring: Continuous measurement of SF6 gas density in electrical switchgear.
  • Early warning system: Immediate alert in case of critical gas loss.
  • Energy efficiency: LoRaWAN-based sensors with ultra-low power consumption.

Our solution is based on LoRaWAN technology, which enables reliable and long-range data transmission even in remote areas. The collected data is transmitted in real time to our cloud-based platform, where it is analyzed and visualized.

With our SF6 gas density monitoring, you benefit from:

  • Increased safety:  Minimization of risks through early detection of gas leaks.
  • Cost savings:  Avoidance of expensive repairs and downtime.
  • Environmental compliance:  Reduction of SF6 emissions through precise monitoring.

Rely on forward-looking technology to ensure the safety and efficiency of your electrical systems. Contact us to learn more about our IoT solutions for SF6 gas density monitoring.
